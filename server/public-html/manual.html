How to use the C-Mail E-Mail Manager

Getting Started
Step 1: Download

Currently we only support Windows. You can downlaod the executeable playing our little seeking game, where you have to find the download link within dozens of ads. Be assured that our software will not contain any advertisment or paywall. After the download, simply start the application.

Step 2: Login

You will be asked to login with your email account. Since we work with IMAP you will have to enable it at your provider. If you need more information how to that, you will find instructions below.

  • GMAIL- Gmail Accounts with 2FA need to use a App-Password to login
  • GMX
  • OUTLOOK/HOTMAIL

Depending on the amount of emails your account holds, you might need to wait a momment, while the system is loading and sorting. The loading screen will keep you up to date about the progress.

Step 3: Manage your Emails

The main windows gives you an overiew over your received emails, grouped by the sender. If it was recognised as newletter you will get the option to unsubscribe. Generally you are able to delete all emails from a sender with the delete button.

FAQ
